Frequently Asked Questions

What warranty do you offer on your services? expand_more

All our custom builds and repairs come with a 1-year parts and labor warranty. We stand by the quality of our work and the components we use.

How long does a custom PC build take? expand_more

Typical turnaround time for a custom PC build is 7-10 business days, depending on component availability and the complexity of the build. We'll provide a more accurate timeline upon consultation.

Can I provide my own parts for a build or upgrade? expand_more

Absolutely! We can work with parts you already own. However, our warranty will only cover the labor for parts provided by you, not the components themselves.
